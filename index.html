The Portrait Series

Taking a black and white picture and creating a story

Description

The Portrait Series is the latest project of Dr. Carletta S. Hurt. The Portrait Series is a three short film series that explores vintage black and white photos and bring them to life. This series looks at photos of influential Black women and transport their energy to now.

A More Perfect Party - Diahann Carroll and Shirley Chisolm
